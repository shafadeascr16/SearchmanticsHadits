Shahih Muslim - Hadits No. 1234

Arabic:
لَا تُؤْمِنُوا حَتَّى تَحَابُّوا.
Translation:
"Do not believe until you love one another."

Shahih Bukhari - Hadits No. 5678

Arabic:
حَبُّ النَّاسِ لِمَا تُحِبُّ.
Translation:
"Love for the people what you love for yourself."
